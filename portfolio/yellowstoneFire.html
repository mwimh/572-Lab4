Reborn in Fire - The Yellowstone Fire of 1988

This map was created to show the extent of destruction caused by the Yellowstone fire of 1988, the largest fire in the Yellowstone area in recorded history. Also, additional information helps users understand why natural wildfire is important to the ecosystem of Yellowstone as well as information about the future risk of wildfire. Also mapped are all wildfires in the last 200 years, showing the magnitude of the fire of 1988 compared to other fires in Yellowstone.
