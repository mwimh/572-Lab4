Worldwide Greenhouse Gas Emissions per Country

This map was created as an exercise in choropleth mapping and propotional symbol mapping. The map shows proportional symbols for the amount of CO2e emitted per year and each country is shaded based on the per capita emission of CO2e. The top ten total and per capita emitters are labeled and their rank is shown for comparison.
