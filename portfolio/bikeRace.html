Terrain Map of Fictional Bicycle Race Through Yellowstone

This map was created for an assigment in generating a terrain map with the premise mapping a route for a bike race similar to the Tour de France. The location chosen was Yellowstone National Park and 21 legs of approximately 100 miles were created and mapped for the race. The hillshade was generated from DEM data and overlaid with land classifictions to create a natural looking basemap over which the race route was presented.
