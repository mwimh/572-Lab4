Source to Sink - Mapping Watersheds in Wisconsin

This interactive map was created as a collaborative final project to map all the medium and large size watersheds in wisconin and to evaluate which watersheds were above and below the selected watershed. Users can toggle different informational layers, search for specific loctions, and click on specific watersheds for more information.
